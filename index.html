James Madison Intermediate School

General Info

James Madison Intermediate School is the best school ever. The teachers are very kind. We have 2 sets of bathrooms, a gym, a trailer, 32 classrooms, a playground, 3 feilds, and a basketball court. We also have 45 teachers in the school. We have very nice events such as Earth Day Events, Mother's Day Flower Events, and Fun Night were we get to watch a movie and eat snacks.



Subjects We Learn

In James Madison Intermediate School we learn Science, Writing, Math, and English. We also learn spanish, gym, art, and music.



Exams In James Madison Intermediate School Takes

In James Madison Intermediate School we take Math Tests, Science Tests, and Vocab Quizzez. In the standard school year we take 2 tests. The Start Strong Assessment and the NJSLA. NJSLA stands for New Jersey Student Learning Assessments. We take the Start Strong in the Start of the School year and the NJSLA in the end of the School year.
